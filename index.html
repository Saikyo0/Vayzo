Vayzo

Want To Watch A Movie With Friends But Fear Low Bandwith? Laggy Internet? Or Even Shitty Quality?

How To Use Vayzo:-

  1. Download The Video File *(skip if you already have the file)
  2. Generate Room And Join It
  3. Make The Other Person Do The Same With Same Room Key
  4. Click "Select Video" And Select The Video
  5. Other Guy Do The Same
  6. Have Fun

Tips:-

  • Is One Of You Out Of Sync? Just Click "Sync" And All of You Will Be Synced
  • Seek The Video After Pausing Then Click Sync (Not Doing That Can Cause Errors)
  • Encountered An Error? Refresh Page, Pause, Seek and ReSync
